How to use

1. Convert your .results file to a .txt file.

On a PC, right click it and 'Open with' Notepad. Click 'Save As' and make sure .txt is selected in the dropdown menu.

On a Mac, right click and 'Open with' TextEdit. Press 'Command ⌘ Shift ⇧ S' to duplicate it and press Save, making sure to click the button 'If no extension provided, use ".txt."'.


2. Go to Upload Files and upload both the IUPred .txt file and the nuclear score .csv file.

Please do not do edit the files from their raw state.

Once this is done, you will be automatically redirected to this page.

3. Press the button below to initiate file conversion.

This will take the .txt and .csv files and convert them into usable data. Depending on your computing power, it may take more or less time.
